# Feed Posts
This folder contains Instagram feed concepts created for Café Picante.

 ## Content Types

### 1. Food Features
Highlight individual dishes, bestsellers, new items, and close-up food photography.

Examples:
- Signature dishes
- Bestsellers
- New menu items
- Desserts

---

### 2. Coffee
Create a consistent series around Café Picante's coffee offerings.

Examples:
- Coffee spotlights
- Hot vs iced coffee
- Coffee-making process

---

### 3. Drinks
Showcase colorful and refreshing drinks through visually appealing photography.

Examples:
- Mocktails
- Fresh juices
- Smoothies
- Seasonal drinks
- New drink launches

---

### 4. Hotpot
Create a distinct but cohesive content series around the hotpot experience.

Examples:
- Hotpot ingredients
- "Build your perfect hotpot"
- Hotpot combinations
- Group dining content
- Step-by-step hotpot experience

---

### 5. Promotional Posts
Used for:

- Discounts
- Student deals
- Bundle offers
- New launches
- Seasonal promotions

---

### 6. Café Experience
Showcase the atmosphere and the people behind the experience.

Examples:

- Customer experiences
- Events
- Behind the scenes
- Reviews
- Café ambience

---

## Visual Direction

All content should follow one consistent visual identity:

- Black, white, brown, beige, and cream color palette
- One consistent primary font
- Consistent photo editing
- Minimal and clean graphic design
- Repeating layouts and design elements

The goal is not for every post to look identical, but for the entire feed to feel like one cohesive brand.
