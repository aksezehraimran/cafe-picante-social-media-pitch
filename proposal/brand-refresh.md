# Brand Refresh

## Goal

Build a premium café page that people want to visit, save, and share.

## Color Palette

- Black
- White
- Brown
- Beige
- Cream

## Typography

One display font.
One clean sans-serif.

Keep it consistent across every post.

## Photography

- Warm lighting
- Shots of food & coffee
- Clean compositions

## Highlights

Replace the current mixed highlight covers with custom icons.

Using one color palette, one font and one illustration style across every highlight for a cleaner, more unique to the brand look.

Examples:
- Coffee 
- Food 
- Hotpot 
- Drinks
- Reviews 
- Events 
- Menu
- Location
- Customer stories reposts
