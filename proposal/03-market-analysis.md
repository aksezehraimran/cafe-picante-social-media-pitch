# Market Analysis

## Market Overview

Café Picante operates in the competitive café and casual dining market, offering coffee, other beverages, food, and hotpot.

Customers increasingly discover cafés and restaurants through social media. A strong and consistent digital presence can help Café Picante attract new customers and remain competitive.

---

## Target Audience

- University students
- Young adults
- Friends and groups
- Families
- Casual diners
- Coffee and food enthusiasts

The content should be visually appealing, relatable, shareable, and relevant to the café experience.

---

## Competitive Landscape

Café Picante competes with:

- Cafés
- Coffee shops
- Restaurants
- Hotpot restaurants
- Casual dining spots

Competitors use social media to promote food, drinks, offers, ambience, and customer experiences.

---

## Market Opportunity

Café Picante offers a diverse menu, including:

- Coffee
- Other beverages
- Food
- Hotpot

This provides a wide range of content opportunities.

However, the current social media presence can be made more visually consistent and recognizable.

A stronger visual identity can help Café Picante stand out from competitors and create stronger brand recognition.

---

## Proposed Competitive Advantage

The proposed strategy focuses on:

- Consistent black, white, brown, beige, and cream colour palette
- One consistent primary font
- High-quality food and drink photography
- Consistent photo editing
- Repeating design elements and layouts
- Content focused on the complete Café Picante experience

The goal is to make the feed instantly recognizable while keeping the content visually interesting.

---

### Café Experience

- Customer experiences
- Reviews

## Social Media Strategy

### Hashtags

Use a combination of:

- Branded hashtags
- Location-based hashtags
- Food-related hashtags
- Niche hashtags

Examples:

#CafePicante  
#HyderabadFood  
#HyderabadEats  
#PakistanFoodies  
#CoffeeLovers  
#Hotpot  
#FoodiePakistan

Hashtags should be adjusted according to the content of each post.

---

### Keywords

Use searchable keywords in captions and on-screen text.

Examples:

- Café in Hyderabad
- Coffee in Hyderabad
- Hotpot in Hyderabad
- Best food in Hyderabad
- Student-friendly café
- Casual dining
- Coffee and food
- Group dining

---

### Trending Sounds

Reels should use a combination of:

- Trending audio
- Original café sounds
- Food preparation sounds
- Customer reactions
- Voiceovers

Trending sounds should be selected based on their current popularity and relevance to the content.

---

### Posting Times

Suggested time periods to test:

- 12:00 PM – 2:00 PM
- 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM
- 8:00 PM – 10:00 PM

Instagram Insights should be used to identify when Café Picante's specific audience is most active.

---

### Content Frequency

Suggested posting schedule:

- 3–5 feed posts per week
- 3–5 reels per week
- Daily stories

The schedule can be adjusted based on engagement and content performance.

---

## Overall Goal

The goal is to build a consistent and recognizable online identity for Café Picante while using social media to showcase its food, coffee, drinks, hotpot, promotions, and overall café experience.

A consistent visual identity combined with relevant content, strategic posting, trending audio, and searchable keywords can help Café Picante improve brand recognition and audience engagement.
